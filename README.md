# Swiggy-food-Delivery-Analytics-Dashboard
Interactive Power BI Dashboard for Swiggy Food Delivery Analytics

> **Interactive Power BI Dashboard for Swiggy Food Delivery Analysis**  
> Analyzing Orders, Revenue, User Behavior, City Performance, and Restaurant Insights using Power BI.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Swiggy Food Delivery Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive business intelligence project built using **Power BI**.

This dashboard analyzes Swiggy's food delivery operations across **4 interactive pages**:

- 🏠 Overview
- 👥 User Performance
- 🗺️ City Overview
- 🍽️ Restaurant Analysis

## 🖥️ Dashboard Pages

---

### 🏠 Page 1 — Overview

![Overview](Screenshots/Overview.png)

The **Overview page** is the executive summary of the entire Swiggy platform. It gives a bird's-eye view of overall business performance with dynamic filters.

**What's on this page:**
- 📦 **KPI Cards** — Total Orders (149K), Users (100K), Revenue ₹725M, Top 10% Customer Revenue
- 🍱 **Food Category Breakdown** — Avg price and total quantity for Veg (₹182), Non-Veg (₹231), and Others
- 📅 **Amount by Year** — Revenue trend from 2017 to 2020
- 🏙️ **Top N City Sales** — Dynamic filter (Top 5 / 10 / 20 / 30 / 100) with bar chart
- 🔘 **Amount vs Quantity Toggle** — Switch between revenue and volume view

---

### 👥 Page 2 — User Performance

![User Performance](Screenshots/User_Performance.png)

The **User Performance page** digs into customer demographics and buying behavior — who is ordering on Swiggy and how.

**What's on this page:**
- 👤 **User Count** — 78K users with gender filter (Male / Female)
- 📅 **Year Slicer** — Filter data by 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020
- 💍 **Sales by Marital Status** — Single users outspend married users
- 💼 **Sale by Occupation** — Employees dominate (53.52%), followed by Self-Employed (30.35%) and Students (13.86%)
- 🎂 **Users by Age** — Peak ordering age group is 25–30 years

---

### 🗺️ Page 3 — City Overview

![City Overview](Screenshots/City_Overview.png)

The **City Overview page** provides a geographic and tabular breakdown of Swiggy's performance across 821 cities in India.

**What's on this page:**
- 🗺️ **India Map Visual** — Geographic distribution of orders across cities
- 📋 **City-wise Table** — City name, Total Sale, Orders, Current Year Sale for every city
- 💰 **Top Cities by Sales** — Tirupati (₹43M), Electronic City Bangalore (₹29M), Baner Pune (₹27M)
- ⭐ **Rating Count by City** — Cities with highest customer engagement
- 🍽️ **Top 5 Cuisines by City** — North Indian & Chinese dominate across all cities

---

### 🍽️ Page 4 — Restaurant Analysis

![Restaurant Analysis](Screenshots/Restaurant_Analysis.png)

The **Restaurant Analysis page** focuses on restaurant-level performance, cuisine popularity, and food type distribution.

**What's on this page:**
- 🥗 **Restaurant by Food Type** — Veg (65%), Non-Veg (34.93%), Others (0.06%)
- 💸 **Sum of Price by Cuisine** — North Indian leads in total revenue across all cuisines
- 🏙️ **Restaurant Count by City** — Bikaner, Indore, Rohtak top the list
- 🍕 **Top 5 Cuisines by Restaurant** — Indian (23.67%), Chinese (18.64%), North Indian (17.62%)
- ⭐ **Rating Count by Restaurant** — Domino's Pizza leads with 409 ratings, followed by Pizza Hut (318) and KFC (306)
